Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply DharaMitra?

Mix 5ml of DharaMitra per liter of water and apply directly to soil or through drip irrigation. For best results, apply during early morning or late evening when soil temperatures are moderate.

What is the recommended application frequency?

Apply DharaMitra every 15-20 days throughout the growing season. For high-value crops or during disease pressure, applications can be made every 10-15 days.

Is DharaMitra compatible with other agricultural inputs?

Yes, DharaMitra is compatible with most fertilizers and biological inputs. However, avoid mixing with chemical fungicides. Allow a 5-7 day interval between fungicide application and DharaMitra treatment.

What is the shelf life of DharaMitra?

DharaMitra has a shelf life of 12 months when stored in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Always check the manufacturing date on the package before use.
